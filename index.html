About Us

We here at LNMIIT are a dynamic group of technical students trying to find some solutions for the betterment of society by the medium of this project under the supervision of Dr. Abhishek Sharma (Faculty E.C.E. @ LNMIIT). We started this project with a vision that it will become a breakthrough in communication with the specially gifted people who use sign language to communicate and hence will result in better understanding and bonding between two communities and also they won't always be needing a translator to communicate their ideas.

We will mainly foocus on conversion to regional language to make it more effective on ground. We are also focusing its on being wearableand portable to increase usbility in daily life. Hope we can contribute to the society by medium of this project. This project is funded DST-SEED div.( Department of science and technology ) so here it is what we have developed for understanding and presenting a platform for better communications.We have tried to make it as easy as possible so that for users it might be handy to use and easy to run.
